Project ecosystem 1 - Visualisation of the human instestinal ecosystem
by Daria Evseeva, Eduardo Vela, Nicolas Brich, Sarah Ertel, Constantin Holzapfel 21.1.2019

Scientists sequence thousands of samples of the human bacterial intestinal ecosystem and collect the metadata from those healthy volunteers to correlate this data to health issues.

This project focuses on ways to visualize this data and their metadata.
The sampled data originates from Lahti et al. (https://www.nature.com/articles/ncomms5344), which is available here: https://datadryad.org/resource/doi:10.5061/dryad.pk75d.

It consists of 1006 microbiome profiles showing the relative abundance of each genus-like taxa. The collected metadata to each profile consists of 9 attributes, where only selected attributes are being visualized.
